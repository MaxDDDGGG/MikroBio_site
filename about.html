About Mikro-Bio Ltd

Mikro-Bio Ltd is a biotechnology company that focuses on developing intelligent automation embedded into diagnostic testing workflows. We are dedicated to creating innovative solutions that help laboratories, manufacturers and healthcare organisations improve accuracy, efficiency and data integrity.

Founded by scientists with hands-on experience in diagnostics, biotechnology and software development, Mikro-Bio Ltd brings together expertise from multiple disciplines to create cutting-edge diagnostic solutions.

Our Flagship Innovation

The MikroBot Platform

Automates the reading and interpretation of diagnostic assays, reducing subjectivity while delivering fast, reliable and traceable results.

Intelligent Engineering

By integrating advanced image analysis with intuitive software, we enable teams to streamline workflows and minimize human error.

Scalable Infrastructure

Our adaptive technology is built to seamlessly support a wide array of test formats, serological assays and custom development pipelines.

Assays in developmental phase
